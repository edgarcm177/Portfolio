Georgia Tech Research & Technical Projects

I started off in this role by cleaning up the dataset of any duplicates and handling missing values with python. Then I got the chance to apply and develop prompts for ChatGpt to turn our unstructured dataset to into a structured one. Then I got to apply text mining on our structured data to prepare it for our current step which is applying hierarchical clustering to identify edge cases in the data. This experience has helped sharpened my ability to work with large-scale data and collaborate on technical research.
Beyond my research, I also built a maze-navigating robot using the iRobot Create 3, which could autonomously detect obstacles and reroute itself. This project allowed me to apply object-oriented programming, asynchronous functions, and real-time decision-making algorithms to a real-world system.
