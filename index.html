About Me

Hey, I'm Edgar Cruz! I'm currently a Computer Science student at Georgia Tech, passionate about software development and problem-solving. Originally from Gainesville, GA, I graduated as the valedictorian of East Hall High School.

At Georgia Tech, I've gotten the chance to be a Research Assistant in the ACT Lab. Where I've utilized LLM's to extract insights from datasets of autonomous vehicle crashes. Through this experience, I’ve honed my data analysis and machine learning skills while collaborating with a team to improve research methodologies. Previously, I worked as a Web Development Intern at Brenau University, where I learned about web accessibility and contributed to improving the university’s online presence. I also spent two years at Walmart, where I enhanced my customer service and problem-solving abilities while improving store operations.

I’m always eager to learn new technologies and improve my skills, whether through research, personal projects, or leadership roles in my community. I'm excited about the future and looking forward to opportunities where I can leverage my adaptability, work ethic, and passion for technology to make an impact!
